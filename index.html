How It Works

1

Build Generators

Invest coins into generators that produce a steady stream of income. The stronger your economy, the bigger your army.

2

Train Soldiers

Spend coins on Troopers, Archers, and Tanks. Each unit counters another — choose your composition wisely.

3

Conquer Districts

March your army to an enemy HQ. Destroy it, capture their flag, absorb their territory. Last district standing wins.

⚔

4-Player Battles

Real-time strategy with 4 districts competing simultaneously. Form alliances, break them, backstab your way to victory.

💰

Economy vs. Military

Every coin is a choice. Invest in generators for long-term power or soldiers for immediate pressure. There's no single right answer.

🌐

Zero Downloads

Play instantly in your browser. Share a link, start a war. No installs, no accounts, no friction.

🤖

Smart AI

Practice against AI opponents with Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty. They scout, adapt, and fight back.
